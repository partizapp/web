Truth or Dare is one of the most popular party games ever. It's simple to learn, needs no equipment, and works with any group size. Here's everything you need to know to play.

## The Basic Rules

1. **Gather your friends** — Truth or Dare works best with 3–10 players.
2. **Take turns** — Go around the circle. Each player chooses Truth or Dare when it's their turn.
3. **Truth** — Answer a personal question honestly. No lying!
4. **Dare** — Complete a challenge. No backing out (unless you use a joker, if your group allows).
5. **Pass** — Some groups allow one pass per game. Decide before you start.

## Truth Questions (Easy to Spicy)

- What's the most embarrassing thing you've done to impress someone?
- Have you ever lied to get out of plans?
- What's your guilty pleasure?
- Who was your childhood crush?
- What's the worst date you've ever been on?

## Dare Ideas (Fun & Safe)

- Do your best impression of someone in the room.
- Sing the chorus of a song of the group's choice.
- Do 10 push-ups.
- Call a friend and say "I have something to tell you" then hang up.
- Eat a spoonful of a random condiment.

## Pro Tips

- **Set boundaries** — Agree on what's off-limits before you start.
- **Keep it fun** — The goal is laughter, not embarrassment.
